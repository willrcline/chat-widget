Webchat Widget Demo

This page demonstrates the webchat widget loaded as a simple script tag.

The widget should appear as a button in the bottom-right corner of the page.

Click the button to open the chat interface and try sending a message!

Usage

To use this widget on your website, simply include:

<script src="webchat-widget.umd.cjs"></script> <script> WebchatWidget.init(); </script>

You can also mount it to a specific container:

<script> WebchatWidget.init('#my-container'); </script>
